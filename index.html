Air Quality Monitoring

Key Air Quality Facts

  • Air Quality Index (AQI) measures how polluted the air is and what associated health effects might be a concern.
  • PM2.5 particles are 30 times smaller than a human hair and can penetrate deep into the lungs.
  • PM10 particles are larger but still harmful, mainly affecting the respiratory system.
  • Ozone (O₃) at ground level is a harmful pollutant that can cause breathing problems.
  • NO₂, SO₂, and CO are gases that contribute to acid rain and respiratory issues.
  • Air pollution causes 7 million premature deaths annually worldwide (WHO, 2022).
  • Children, elderly, and people with respiratory conditions are most vulnerable.

How AQI is Calculated

The Air Quality Index is calculated using a formula that considers multiple pollutants:

AQI Formula:
AQI = max(AQI_PM2.5, AQI_PM10, AQI_O3, AQI_NO2, AQI_SO2, AQI_CO)

Each pollutant has its own sub-index calculated as:

AQI_pollutant = [(I_high - I_low) / (C_high - C_low)] × (C - C_low) + I_low

Where:

  • I_high, I_low = AQI breakpoint values
  • C_high, C_low = Pollutant concentration breakpoints
  • C = Actual pollutant concentration

How This Web App Monitors Air Quality

This application uses multiple data sources to provide accurate air quality information:

1
API Integration
Connects to weather APIs (like OpenWeatherMap) to fetch real-time pollutant data
2
Data Processing
Processes raw pollutant concentrations (PM2.5, PM10, O₃, etc.) using EPA formulas
3
AQI Calculation
Calculates AQI using the highest pollutant sub-index as the overall AQI
4
Health Assessment
Provides health advisories based on AQI levels and vulnerable groups
5
Data Storage
Stores historical data in MongoDB for trend analysis and predictions

Monitoring Stations

Air quality data comes from:

  • Government Monitors: EPA-certified stations with precise instruments
  • Community Sensors: Low-cost sensors providing hyper-local data
  • Satellite Data: NASA satellites for global coverage
  • Weather APIs: Real-time integration with weather services
